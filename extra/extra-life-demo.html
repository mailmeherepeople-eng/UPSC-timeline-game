How the mechanic works
Earning: Reach a 5-streak and the shield icon lights up with a pop animation + earn banner. One per game — no re-earning after it's spent.

Absorbing a hit: Get a wrong answer while the shield is active → shield shatters, gold flash fills the screen, a toast confirms the save. Hearts stay intact. Streak resets as normal.

UI placement: Lives on the right of the header, shield sits next to them — grayed out until earned, glowing gold while active, faded grey once spent.
