Quick Guide

How to Play

Step 1 of 3
Press & Hold the Card
A card appears showing a historical event, but no date. To move it, press and hold the card for a moment until it lifts, then drag it.

⚠️ Simply tapping or clicking won't work. You must press, hold, then drag.

Step 2 of 3
Place It in the Right Era
The timeline shows events in chronological order: oldest on the left, most recent on the right. Every card already on the timeline shows its date.

Your job is to guess when the new event happened and drop it in the correct position. For example, if the timeline shows 322 BCE on the left and 1947 CE on the right, and your card is the Battle of Plassey. You know it happened in colonial times, so you'd drop it closer to the right, before 1947.

A green line lights up in the gap as you hover. That's where your card will land. Drop it there.

At the very start or end of the timeline, the drop area is extra wide. Just drag into the open space and release.

Step 3 of 3
See the Result
Correct: the card joins the timeline in green and the date is revealed. Your score goes up.

Wrong: the card still joins the timeline but turns red, and you lose a life. Three wrong and it's game over.

The timeline stays after the game ends so you can review every placement.
